*Progressive educator focused on inspiring the whole-child to pursue a meaningful life of learning and self-confidence by instilling a personal understanding of resiliency, grit, and creativity. Through continuous personal reflection, networking, and a growth mindset, my teaching is rooted in a flexible, adaptive model that draws on the whole-child philosophy, an integration of technology and an understanding of data. I believe in innovation anywhere, and the essential nature of working in collaboration.*
